Coheed and Cambria fan site!

Bio:

Coheed and Cambria[a] is an American progressive rock band from Nyack, New York, formed in 1995.Most of Coheed and Cambria's albums are concept albums based on a science fiction storyline called The Amory Wars, a series written by Claudio Sanchez, which has been transcribed into a series of comic books as well as a full-length novel

Reasons I like Coheed and Cambria:

  • They write a comic book to go with their music
  • They have been one of my favorite shows to see live
  • I love the artwork created in both visual and auditory mediums
